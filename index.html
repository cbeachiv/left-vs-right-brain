The Story

Two minds.
One conversation.

Left vs Right Brain started in 2016, when podcasts were still finding their footing. Greg and Chase had a simple idea: talk about the topics shaping technology — but through two fundamentally different mindsets.

Greg is the analytical one. Chase is the creative. They don't disagree — they just approach everything from different angles. What started as casual conversations over drinks became something more when AI started changing everything.

In early February, when tools like OpenClaw started gaining traction and both hosts began building their own AI projects, the podcast found its focus: helping people navigate the world of AI from wherever they're standing.
